The goal of the game is to beat the dealer by having a hand value as close to 21 as possible without exceeding it.

Number cards (2-10) are worth their face value, face cards (Jack, Queen, King) are worth 10, and Aces can be worth 1 or 11, depending on your hand. Aces automatically take the value that benefits your hand the most, so they will default to 1 if choosing 11 would cause you to bust.

The game begins with the dealer serving two initial cards to both the player and themselves. You won't see the dealer's second card initially.

You choose to 'Hit' to receive another card or 'Stand' to keep your current hand. It is the dealer's turn when you stand.

You win the game if your hand value is closer to 21 than the dealer's, without going over 21. If your hand value equals 21, you win immediately. You lose if your hand value exceeds 21 or if the dealer's hand is closer to 21 than yours.

It can also be a tie if your hand has the same value as the dealer's hand.

Have fun and good luck! ♥️
